Iceland

Europe

"Where fire and ice dance together."

Population: 382,300 (2022)

Latitude: 65° 00' N Longitude: 18° 00' W

Capital City: Reikiavik

Psssst: small tip, you can scroll to the right inside the image box to look at more pictures :D

Iceland is a country from Europe, it's an island that's surrounded by the Nordic Sea shared with Norway, Greenland, and the United Kingdom. It's main appeal is the closeness to nature itself, it's an island located in the middle of two tectonic plates: The North American Plate and Eurasian Plate. Creating unique glaciers and volcanoes, which allows for fire and ice to combine altogether and form wonders, almost magically. Iceland's capital represents the main turistic place for people all over the world, also, it's one of the safest countries to visit. Black Sands beaches are a must visit in Iceland, Reynisfjara being the most popular one for visits.

Fun Facts

  • Iceland only has one native land mammal, which is the Artic Fox.
  • The World's largest puffin colony is in Iceland
  • Mosquitoes does not exist in this land.
  • Sheep can roam freely around the country in summer.

Norway

Europe

"Where I want to see the dancing of multicolored sky lights."

Population: 5,457,000 (2022)

Latitude: 62° 00' N Longitude: 10° 00' E

Capital City: Oslo

Psssst: small tip, you can scroll to the right inside the image box to look at more pictures :D

Norway is known by their renowned natural landscapes, such as the most amazing fjords (just like in Frozen :D), snow copped mountains, and breathtaking forests. Norway has a smooth blend between the modern world with their viking history, inviting you to city like Oslo and Bergen. Norway is one of the most beautiful countries to have this one-of-a-kind experience.

Fun Facts

  • One of the world's happiest places.
  • Norwegians invented skiing around 4000 years ago.
  • Norwegians read more than any other population in the world.
  • Norway has the most gold, silver and bronze medals for Winter Olympic performance, making it the most accomplished country in the winter games.
